Terms and Privacy Policy

Introduction
The Trustee for Full Horizon Drones Trust regards customer privacy as an important part of our relationship with our customers. The following privacy policy applies to all The Trustee for Full Horizon Drones Trust users, and conforms to Internet privacy standards.
If you have questions or concerns regarding this statement, you should first contact the Full Horizon Drones team on 0418 733 425.

All correspondence may also be collected and stored, particularly in regard to sales, support and accounts, including Email.

If you have any questions about security on our Website, you can email us at training@fullhorizondrones.net.au

Additional training
All Remote Pilot Licence training is competency based. Full Horizon Drones schedules courses based on an anticipated practical experience required. On occasion, additional training will be required to acquire the required competency.

Initial Remote Pilot Licence training
Full Horizon Drones schedules an anticipated 5 hours of practical flight experience. Should the required competency not be reached within this time, additional training will be scheduled free of charge at Full Horizon Drones standard scheduled locations at the convenience of Full Horizon Drones. It is anticipated that scheduled remedial training will proceed as planned though Full Horizon Drones does not guarantee availability for free of charge remedial training. Additional fees may be paid to guarantee training dates.

Remote Pilot Licence upgrades
It is expected that students attending a Remote Pilot Licence upgrade course have accumulated flight experience of at least 5 hours.
Upgrades to a new aircraft type are planned as per an initial course. Should the required competency not be reached, additional training will be scheduled free of charge at Full Horizon Drones standard scheduled locations at the convenience of Full Horizon Drones. It is anticipated that scheduled remedial training will proceed as planned though Full Horizon Drones does not guarantee availability for free of charge remedial training. Additional fees may be paid to guarantee training dates.
Students conducting upgrades on the same aircraft type are expected to meet the competencies of the lower licence category. This will be assessed prior to conducting upgrade training. Should existing competency not reach the lower licence category, remedial training will be conducted during the scheduled training but is not guaranteed during that session. Additional fees may apply if additional training is requested to meet the lower licence category before commencing the upgrade training.
