From Advertising to Drone Training

Full Horizon Pty Ltd was establish in 2006 in the advertising industry using billboard sign. In mid 2015, we started in the UAV / Drone industry running courses for future UAV / Drone pilots issuing RePL's.

Full Horizon Drones was then establish in mid 2017, offering our services in the Surveying, Mapping, Inspection and Training / Licensing areas.

Full Horizon Drones is now approved by CASA to conduct RePL training and operates under the new CASA regulations, ReOC 6478.

Meet John Kirby

Managing Director of Full Horizon Pty Ltd, Johno, has been in the Aviation Industry for over 28 years as a Commercial / Airline Pilot flying in single and twin engine aircraft. He has worked for charter companies doing charters, freight runs and for larger companies in airline operations. Has also worked for the (RFDS) flying doctors around on medivac operations.
